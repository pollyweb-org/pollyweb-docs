1. **What is the .Filter function?**

    `{.Filter}`
    * is a [{Function} üêç](<../üìÉ basics/Function üêç.md>) 
    * that filters a list.

    ---
    <br/>

1. **What's the .Filter syntax?**

    ```yaml
    .Filter($list, [filters])
    ```

    Inputs|Type|Details | Example
    |-|-|-|-
    |`$list`| list | Items to be filtered | `1,2` `{A:1},{A:2}`
    |`[filters]`| list | Boolean evaluations | `.Is(2)` `A:2`

    ---
    <br/>

1. **What are example outputs?**

    |Scenario|List|Conditions|Output
    |-|-|-|-
    |Empty list| `[]` | `A:1` | `[]`
    |Empty filters| `{A:1}` | `[]` | üö´ Blocked
    |Value lists| `1,2,3` | `.IsIn(1,3)` | `1,3`
    || `1,2,3` | `.Is(5)` | `[]`
    |Object lists| `{A:1},{A:2}` | `A:1` | `A:1`
    || `{A:1},{A:2}` | `A:3` | `[]`

    ---
    <br/>
   
1. **What's an example of .Filter?**

    Consider a list `$items`.

    ```yaml
    # Items
    | ID | Price | SupID  |
    | -- | ----- | ------ |
    |  1 |    10 |      A |
    |  2 |    20 |      X |
    |  3 |    30 |      X |
    |  4 |    40 |      X |
    ```

    |Input 1 | Input 2 |Output
    |-|-|-
    |`$items` |`SupID:X` | Items 2, 3, and 4
    |`$items` |`SupID.Is(X)` <br/> `Price.AtLeast(30)`  | Items 3 and 4
