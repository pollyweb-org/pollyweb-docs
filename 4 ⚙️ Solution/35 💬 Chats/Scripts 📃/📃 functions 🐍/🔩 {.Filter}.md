1. **What is the .Filter function?**

1. **What's the .Filter syntax?**

    ```yaml
    .Filter($list, [conditions])
    ```

    Inputs|Type|Details
    |-|-|-
    |`$list`| list | Items to be filtered
    |`[conditions]`| list | Boolean evaluations

    ---
    <br/>

1. **What's an example of .Filter?**

    Consider a list `$items`.

    ```yaml
    # Items
    | ID | Price | SupID  |
    | -- | ----- | ------ |
    |  1 |    10 |      A |
    |  2 |    20 |      X |
    |  3 |    30 |      X |
    |  4 |    40 |      X |
    ```

    |Input 1 | Input 2 |Output
    |-|-|-
    |`$items` |`SupID:X` | Items 2, 3, and 4
    |`$items` |`SupID.Is(X)` <br/> `Price.AtLeast(30)`  | Items 3 and 4
