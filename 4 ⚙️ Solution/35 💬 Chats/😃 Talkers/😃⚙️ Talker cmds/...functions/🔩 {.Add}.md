1. **What are usage examples?**

    | Type      | Task  | Arguments   | Result
    |-|-|-|-
    | `Math`    | Sum   | `1, 2, 3` | `6`
    |           | Subtract | `4, -1, -1` | `2`
    | `Time`    | Sum    | `.Now, 1 hour` | -
    |           | Subtract | `.Today, 1 day` | -

    | `Objects` | Merge  | `{A:1},{B:2}` | `{1,2,3,4}`
