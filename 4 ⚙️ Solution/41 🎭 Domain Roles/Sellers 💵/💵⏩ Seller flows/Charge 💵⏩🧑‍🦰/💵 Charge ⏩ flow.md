# 💵👉🧑‍🦰 Charge

## About

- Initiated by: [Seller 💵](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>)

## Definitions

- ♌ Charge ID

    - Type: UUID
    - Example: `fa6ecd80-0662-4428-a6d4-aa63d33dc7a8`
    - Definition: unique ID of the transaction, generated by [Seller 💵 domain](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>).
    - Purpose: for the [Seller 💵 domain](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>) to identify the transaction received by the [Collector 🏦 helper](<../../../../45 🤲 Helper domains/Collectors 🏦/🏦📨 Collector msgs/Collect 💳🐌🏦/🏦 Collect 🐌 msg.md>) once the [Payer 💳 domain](<../../../Payers/💳🎭 Payer role.md>) pays for it.

- ♌ Charge

    - Type: object
    - Example:
        - Charge ID: `<charge-uuid@seller>`
        - Amount: `12.34`
        - Currency: `EUR`
        - Operation: `DEBIT`
        - [Collectors 🏦](<../../../../45 🤲 Helper domains/Collectors 🏦/🏦 Collector/🏦🤲 Collector helper.md>): [ `revolut.dom`, `paypal.dom` ]
            

## Steps

- [Seller 💵 domain](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>) charges [Wallet 🧑‍🦰](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>)

    - [Seller 💵 domain](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>) → [Charge @ Broker 💵🐌🤵 ](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Brokers 🤵/🤵📨 Broker msgs/Payments 💵 Charge 💵🐌🤵/🤵 Charge 🐌 msg.md>)
    - [Broker 🤵 domain](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Brokers 🤵/🤵/🤵 Broker 🤲 helper.md>) → [Charge @ Notifier 🤵💵🐌📣](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Notifiers 📣/📣📨 Notifier msgs/Payments 💳 Charge 🤵🐌📣/📣 Charge 🐌 msg.md>)

- [Wallet 🧑‍🦰 app](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>) pays [Seller 💵 domain](<../../💵 Seller /💵🎭 Seller role.md>)

    - [Wallet 🧑‍🦰 app](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>) → [Endorse @ Payer 🧑‍🦰🐌💳](<../../../Payers/💳📨 Payer msgs/Endorse 🧑‍🦰🐌💳/💳 Endorse 🐌 msg.md>)
    - [Payer 💳 agent](<../../../Payers/💳🎭 Payer role.md>) → [Collect @ Collector 💳🐌🏦](<../../../../45 🤲 Helper domains/Collectors 🏦/🏦📨 Collector msgs/Collect 💳🐌🏦/🏦 Collect 🐌 msg.md>)
    - [Collector 🏦](<../../../../45 🤲 Helper domains/Collectors 🏦/🏦 Collector/🏦🤲 Collector helper.md>) → [Paid @ Seller 🏦🐌💵](<../../💵📨 Seller msgs/Paid 🏦🐌💵/💵 Paid 🐌 msg.md>)
