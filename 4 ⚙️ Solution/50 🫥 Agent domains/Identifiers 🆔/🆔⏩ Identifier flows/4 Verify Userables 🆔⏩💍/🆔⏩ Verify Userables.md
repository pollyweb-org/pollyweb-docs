> Part of [Identifier 🆔 domains](<../../🆔 Identifier agent/🆔 Identifier 🫥 agent.md>)

## FAQ


1. **How to verify someone else's Userable?**

    Yes. 
    * A [Wallet 🧑‍🦰 app](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>) owned by person `A` can authenticate a person `B` in a [Chat 💬](<../../../../35 💬 Chats/Chats 💬/💬 Chat.md>) when the [Chat's Host 🤗 domain](<../../../../41 🎭 Domain Roles/Hosts 🤗/🤗 Host role/🤗🎭 Host role.md>) invites the [Identifier 🆔 vault](<../../🆔 Identifier agent/🆔 Identifier 🫥 agent.md>) of person `B` into the [Chat 💬](<../../../../35 💬 Chats/Chats 💬/💬 Chat.md>).
    * This is particularly useful when working with [Userable 💍 things](<../../../../25 🔆 Locators/Userables 💍/💍💠 Userable thing.md>) where the owner's identity needs to be confirmed on [payments 🤝 scenarios](<../../../../25 🔆 Locators/Userables 💍/💍⏩ Userable flows/💍💳 Pay salesperson.md>) and [item recovery 🤝 scenarios](<../../../../25 🔆 Locators/Userables 💍/💍⏩ Userable flows/💍📱 Senior user.md>) via another [Wallet 🧑‍🦰 app](<../../../../20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>).
