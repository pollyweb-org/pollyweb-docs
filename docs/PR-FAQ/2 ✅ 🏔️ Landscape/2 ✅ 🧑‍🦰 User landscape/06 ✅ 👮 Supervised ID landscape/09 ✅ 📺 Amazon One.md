In 2024, Amazon released the following video, titled "Amazon One launches an app to make sign-up for its palm-recognition service even easier".

- The video introduces Amazon One, a contactless identity service that allows users to enter, identify, and pay using only their palm. 
- The Amazon One app lets customers sign up for the service by simply taking a photo of their palm, making the in-store experience seamless even for first-time users.
- The solution is widely used in gated access for gym members in the US.
