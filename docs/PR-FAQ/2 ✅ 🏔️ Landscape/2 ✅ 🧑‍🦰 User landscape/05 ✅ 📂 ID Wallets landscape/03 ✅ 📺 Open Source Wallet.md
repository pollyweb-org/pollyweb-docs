In 2023, The Linux Foundation published the following video, titled *"LF Research | Why the World Needs an Open Source Digital Wallet Right Now"*.

- The video discusses the rapid digitization of physical wallets to manage digital assets, such as currency and concert tickets. 
- It emphasizes the need for an open-source digital wallet to ensure interoperability, security, and user control over personal data.
