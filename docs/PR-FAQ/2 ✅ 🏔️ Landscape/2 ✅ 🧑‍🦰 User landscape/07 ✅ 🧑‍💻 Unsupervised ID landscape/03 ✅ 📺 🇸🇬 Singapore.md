In 2020, the CCTV Video News Agency released the following video, titled *"Singapore Adopts Facial Verification System for National Identity Program"*.

- The video discusses the Singapore government's adoption of a facial verification system for its national identity program. 
- It shows the adoption of unsupervised remote face biometrics with color-based liveness checks.
- It highlights the benefits of this system, such as reducing the burden on businesses and government agencies to collect and store biometric data. 
- It also emphasizes the importance of user consent and data minimization in the implementation of this technology.
