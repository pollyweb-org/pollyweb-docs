In 2021, the Connected Health Branch of the United States Defense Health Agency (DHA) published the following video, titled *"Personal Health Record (PHR) for Patients"*.

- The video is about Personal Health Record (PHR) for Patients. 
- PHRs are owned and maintained by the patient, giving them control over their health information. 
- PHRs can be linked to electronic health records (EHRs) or used as standalone tools. 
- The video highlights the benefits of using a PHR, such as having a centralized tracking system for managing appointments and vaccinations, improved communication with doctors, and compatibility with wearable health technologies.
