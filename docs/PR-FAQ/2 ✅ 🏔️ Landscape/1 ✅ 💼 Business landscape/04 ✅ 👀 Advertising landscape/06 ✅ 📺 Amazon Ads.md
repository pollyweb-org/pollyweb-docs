In 2023, Amazon Seller University published the following video, titled "Introduction to Amazon Ads"*.
    
- The video explains how Amazon Ads work from the perspective of advertisers. 
- Amazon has grown into a key player in digital advertising, using customer data from its active users to offer targeted ads on its platform. 
- Amazon sells ad space on its Amazon e-commerce site, its streaming services (Prime Video, Freevee, and Twitch), and its Amazon DSP (Demand-Side Platform).
