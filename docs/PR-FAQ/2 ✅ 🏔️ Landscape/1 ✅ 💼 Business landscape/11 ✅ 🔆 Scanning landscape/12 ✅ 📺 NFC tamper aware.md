In 2023, Seritag NFC Tags (a UK-based NFC tag provider) published the following video, titled *"NFC Tamper Tags - A 90 Second Product Intro"*.

- The video introduces two main types of NFC tamper tags: tamper fail and tamper detect. 
- Tamper fail tags break when tampered with, while tamper detect tags change their URL. 
- These tags can be used to enhance product security and prevent counterfeiting.
