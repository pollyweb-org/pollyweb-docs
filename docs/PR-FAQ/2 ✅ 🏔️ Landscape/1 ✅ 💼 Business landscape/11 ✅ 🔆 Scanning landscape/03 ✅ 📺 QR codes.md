In 2013, Tight Designs (a U.S.-based design studio) published the following video, titled *"QR Codes Explained by Common Craft"*.

- It highlights how QR codes can be scanned by smartphones to access information about physical objects, such as websites, videos, or social media profiles, making the physical world more interactive.
