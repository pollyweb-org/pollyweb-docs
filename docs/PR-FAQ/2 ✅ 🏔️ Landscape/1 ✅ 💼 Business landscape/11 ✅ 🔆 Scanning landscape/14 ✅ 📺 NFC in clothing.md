In 2024, Seritag NFC Tags (a UK-based NFC tag provider) published the following video, titled *"Garment NFC Tags Explained - How to use NFC in Clothing"*.

- The video explains how to use NFC technology in clothing. 
- It focuses on two types of NFC tags: disc tags and garment labels. 
- The video demonstrates how to apply these tags to garments using different methods, including integration with patches and using a two-part method with vinyl patches.
