1. **How to get a wheelchair at an airport?**

    Users can then present their disability token to an airline when buying a ticket. 

    - The airline will then performs additional validations (e.g., size and type of wheelchair, special needs) and issues a new token for airline accessibility with details about the needs of the user. 
    - The user can now present the airline accessibility token in airports and future airline journeys.

1. **How to claim accessibility at an airport (e.g., a wheelchair)?**

    Users can bind to their health-authority Vault, share their identity Vault, and download an accessibility token. 

    - When acquiring a ticket with the airline in a chat, users share their accessibility token with an in-chat identity validation. 
    - To simplify subsequent journeys, the airline can issue a second token with additional airline details about the accessibility (e.g., wheelchair dimensions) which can be shared with other airlines. 
    - At the airport, users present one of the tokens, either with an online wallet or an offline QR/NFC.
