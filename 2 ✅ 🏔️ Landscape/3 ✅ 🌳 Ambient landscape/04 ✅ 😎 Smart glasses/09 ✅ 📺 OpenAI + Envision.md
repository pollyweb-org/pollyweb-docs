In 2023, Business Insider (a U.S.-based financial and business news website) published the following video, titled *"Smartglasses Use ChatGPT To Help The Blind And Visually Impaired | 5G Playbook"*.

* The video showcased Envision Glasses, AI-powered smartglasses designed to assist blind and visually impaired individuals. 
* These glasses utilized ChatGPT and Google Glass hardware to interpret visual information, converting it into speech. 
* Enhanced by widespread 5G networks, they offered rapid processing for a seamless user experience.
