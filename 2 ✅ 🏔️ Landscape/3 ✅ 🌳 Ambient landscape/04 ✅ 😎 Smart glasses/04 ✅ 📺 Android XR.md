In 2024, Android Developers (a Google team) published the following video, titled *"XR Unlocked '24 in under 8 minutes"*.

* The video summarized the key announcements from XR Unlocked 2024, where Google introduced Android XR, a new operating system for extended reality devices like headsets and smart glasses. 
* Developed in partnership with Samsung, Android XR integrates Google's Gemini AI to enhance user experiences. 
* The platform supports immersive versions of Google apps and existing Android apps from the Play Store.
