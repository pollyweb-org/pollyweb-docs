In 2023, Apple published the following video, titled *"Introducing Apple Vision Pro"*.

* The video showcases Apple Vision Pro, a spatial computer that seamlessly blends digital content with the physical world. 
* It introduces a three-dimensional user interface controlled by eyes, hands, and voice, allowing users to interact with apps beyond traditional display boundaries.
