In 2023, Arm (UK-based semiconductor company) published the following video, titled *"What is Ambient Intelligence?"*.

- The video discusses the concept of ambient intelligence, which refers to an environment where computing is seamlessly integrated into everyday objects and spaces. 
- It explores how this technology can create personalized and intelligent experiences in various settings, such as homes, offices, and factories.
