In 2022, Connectivity Standards Alliance (a U.S.-based standards development organization) published the following video, titled *"Matter Network Transport - Connectivity Standards Alliance"*.

- The video explains Matter, a new industry standard that allows smart devices from different brands to work together seamlessly. 
- It discusses the different network transports supported by Matter, including Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and Thread, and how they enable interoperability and flexibility in smart home ecosystems.
