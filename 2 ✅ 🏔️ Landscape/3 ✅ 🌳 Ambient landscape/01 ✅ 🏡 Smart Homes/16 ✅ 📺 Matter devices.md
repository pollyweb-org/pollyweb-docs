In 2023, the Connectivity Standards Alliance (a U.S.-based standards development organization) published the following video, titled *"Matter Demo: New Experience of Interoperability Across Ecosystems [English]*".

- The video demonstrates Matter, an open standard for IoT devices. 
- It showcases interoperability between devices from different brands and ecosystems, including Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa. 
- The demo highlights features like multi-admin, device binding, and scene control.
