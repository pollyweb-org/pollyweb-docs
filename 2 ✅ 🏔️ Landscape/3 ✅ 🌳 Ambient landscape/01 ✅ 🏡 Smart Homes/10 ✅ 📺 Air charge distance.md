In 2022, Powercast (a U.S.-based power-tech company) published the following video, titled *"Powered by Powercast - Episode 9 - Long Distance Power Transfer"*.

- The video discusses the company's recent achievement of powering a battery-less Bluetooth beacon from over 120 feet away using their commercially available Power Spot transmitter. 
- This breakthrough enables the placement of IoT sensors in previously inaccessible locations, leading to increased efficiency and cost savings in various applications.
