In 2015, VINCI Energies (a French energy and IT company) published the following video, titled *"What is a smart city?"*.

- The video defined smart cities as urban areas utilizing digital technology to enhance infrastructure, services, and residents' quality of life. 
- It highlighted the integration of data and communication systems to optimize energy use, transportation, and public services.
