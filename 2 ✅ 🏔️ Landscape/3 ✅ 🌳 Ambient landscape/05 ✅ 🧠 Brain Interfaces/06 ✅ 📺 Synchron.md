In 2024, Synchron (a U.S.-based neurotechnology company) published the following video, titled *"How Synchron's BCI Works"*.

- The video explained how Synchron's brain-computer interface (BCI) works.  
- It shows the insertion of a stent-like device into a blood vessel near the brain.  
- Signals are transmitted wirelessly from the device to a computer, enabling users to control digital devices with their thoughts.
