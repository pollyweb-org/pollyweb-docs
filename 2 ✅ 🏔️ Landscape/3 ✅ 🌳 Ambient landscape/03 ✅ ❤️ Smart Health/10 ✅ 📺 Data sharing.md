In 2025, the Ohio State Wexner Medical Center (a U.S.-based academic medical center) published the following video, titled *"Many don't share heart data from smartwatches | Ohio State Medical Center"*.

- The video is about the importance of sharing heart data from smartwatches with your doctor. 
- A national survey found that many people use devices to monitor their heart health, but few share the data with their doctor.
