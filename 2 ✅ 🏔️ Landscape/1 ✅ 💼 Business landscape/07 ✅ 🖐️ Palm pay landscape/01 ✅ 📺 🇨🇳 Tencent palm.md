In 2023, South China Morning Post released the following video, titled *"Pay with your palm: Tencent launches new payment method in China"*.

- The video explains that Tencent has launched a new payment method for WeChat Pay users, who simply need to scan their hands at palm print recognition machines. 
- The technology allows users to pay for rides on Beijing's airport express line and shop at the Shenzhen University.
