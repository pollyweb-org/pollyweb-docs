In 2024, CNBC (a U.S.-based news channel) published the following video, titled *"How Amazon Is Trying To Get Rid Of Checkout Lines At Stores"*.

- CNBC's video explores Amazon's "Just Walk Out" technology, which allows shoppers to bypass checkout lines. 
- The video discusses the technology's development, its deployment in various settings, and Amazon's recent pivot towards selling the technology to third-party retailers. 
- It also highlights challenges like data privacy concerns and the high cost of implementation.
