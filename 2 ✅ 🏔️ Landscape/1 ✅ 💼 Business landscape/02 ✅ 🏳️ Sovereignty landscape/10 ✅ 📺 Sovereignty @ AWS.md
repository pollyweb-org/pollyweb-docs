In 2024, Amazon Web Services (AWS) published the following video, titled *"AWS European Sovereign Cloud launching by the end of 2025"*.

- The video describes AWS plans to launch its European Sovereign Cloud by late 2025, addressing data residency, sovereignty, and compliance needs. 
- Independent of non-European access, it ensures European control over data while offering AWS's advanced cloud services tailored for the region.
