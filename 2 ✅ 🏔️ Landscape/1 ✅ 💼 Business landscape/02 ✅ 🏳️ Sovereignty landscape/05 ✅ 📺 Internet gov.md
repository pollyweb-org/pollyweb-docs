In 2018, the MAPPING Awareness Campaign (a Youtube channel from India that promotes awareness on internet governance) published the following video, titled *"What is Internet Governance?"*.

- The video talks about Internet Governance, which is the set of rules and policies that govern the internet. 
- It discusses the different stakeholders involved in internet governance, such as governments, internet service providers, and civil society. 
- It also highlights the challenges of regulating the internet, such as cyber crime and privacy.
