In 2022, Day Air Credit Union (a U.S.-based credit union) published the following video, titled *"Don't Get Quished"*.

- The video discusses a new type of phishing attack called "quishing" (from QR phishing), where cyber criminals embed malicious links within QR codes. 
- These codes can be found on various surfaces, tricking victims into visiting fraudulent websites and potentially compromising their personal information.
