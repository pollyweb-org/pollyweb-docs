In 2021, Seritag NFC Tags (a UK-based NFC tag provider) published the following video, titled *"What is an NFC Tag?"*.

- It describes the components of an NFC tag, including the chip, antenna, and substrate. 
- It also discusses the process of encoding data onto an NFC tag and the different formats in which NFC tags are available.
