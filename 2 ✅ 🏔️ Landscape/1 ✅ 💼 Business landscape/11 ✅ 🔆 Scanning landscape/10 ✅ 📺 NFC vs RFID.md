In 2022, Eye on Tech (a U.S.-based tech-focused YouTube channel) published the following video, titled *"NFC vs. RFID: What's the Difference?"*.

- The video compares and contrasts NFC and RFID technologies. 
- It explains that RFID is a broader technology for wireless data transmission over longer distances, while NFC is a subset of RFID designed for short-range communication and secure transactions like contactless payments.
