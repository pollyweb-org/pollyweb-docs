In 2022, Seritag NFC Tags (a UK-based NFC tag provider) published the following video, titled *"NFC Tag Authentication Explained - An NFC Pro Tutorial from Seritag".

- The video explains how authentication NFC tags work, focusing on the difference between standard and authentication chips. 
- It details how authentication chips generate unique dynamic codes on each scan, enhancing counterfeit protection. 
- The video also discusses three main ways to use authentication NFC tags: with an API, a redirect system, and a direct response system.
