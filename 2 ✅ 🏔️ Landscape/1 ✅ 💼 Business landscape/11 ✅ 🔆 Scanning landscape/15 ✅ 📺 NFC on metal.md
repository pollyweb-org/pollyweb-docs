In 2024, Seritag NFC Tags (a UK-based NFC tag provider) published the following video, titled *"NFC On-Metal Tags Explained"*.

- The video explains why NFC tags don't work on metal surfaces and provides solutions. 
- It discusses how metal interferes with the electromagnetic induction needed for NFC tag activation. 
- The video presents two solutions: using a physical barrier between the tag and metal, and using specialized "unmetal" tags with a ferrite layer.
