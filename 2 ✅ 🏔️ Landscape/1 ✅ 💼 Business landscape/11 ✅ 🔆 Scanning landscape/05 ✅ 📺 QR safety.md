In 2024,  ABC News (a U.S.-based news organization) published the following video, titled *"FBI issues new warning about QR code scams"*.

- The video discusses the rise of QR code scams, as reported by the U.S. Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), where scammers place fake QR codes over legitimate ones in parking garages and other public areas. 
- These fake codes can redirect users to malicious websites, steal personal information, and install malware on their phones. 
- The FBI advises users to be vigilant and report any suspicious QR codes.
