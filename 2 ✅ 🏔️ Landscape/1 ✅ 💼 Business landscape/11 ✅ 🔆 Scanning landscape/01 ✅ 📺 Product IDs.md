In 2023, Blue Bite (a U.S.-based marketing company) published the following video, titled *"What Are Digital Product IDs?"*.

- The video explains how Digital Product IDs (DPIDs) can benefit brands. 
- DPIDs provide unique identifiers for products, enabling personalized experiences, improved supply chain tracking, and enhanced consumer engagement. 
- This allows brands to build stronger customer relationships and combat issues like counterfeiting and gray market sales.
