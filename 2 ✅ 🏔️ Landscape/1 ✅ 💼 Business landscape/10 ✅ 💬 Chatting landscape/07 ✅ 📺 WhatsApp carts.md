In 2021, WhatsApp released the following video, titled *"Making it Easier to Shop on WhatsApp with Carts"*.

- It shows how Meta allows WhatsApp users to shop within the context of a chat with a business.
- The feature was launched in 2020 inside WhatsApp Business in Brazil and India.
- It allows consumers to talk to businesses, browse catalogs, and pay for purchases in real-time without leaving the chat window.
