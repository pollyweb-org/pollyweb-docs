In 2021, WhatsApp release the following video, titled *"How to send money using QR code on WhatsApp"*.

- The video explains how WhatsApp Payments in India enables users to send and receive money directly within the app, utilizing the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) system. 
- This feature allows for seamless bank-to-bank transfers without any fees.
- To make a payment using a QR code, users can scan the recipient's UPI QR code through WhatsApp to initiate the transaction. 
- This method is particularly useful for paying merchants or individuals in person.
