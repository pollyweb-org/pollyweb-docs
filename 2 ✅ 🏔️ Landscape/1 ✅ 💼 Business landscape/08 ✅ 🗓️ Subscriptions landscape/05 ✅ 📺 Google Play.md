This 2023 video from Google, titled *"Selling subscriptions on Google Play"*, explains how subscriptions work from the perspective of an Android app developer.

- On Google Play, app developers can offer subscription-based services for apps, games, and digital content on Android devices.
- On Google Pay, businesses can integrate their payment processor (e.g., Braintree, PayPal, [Stripe](#Stripe)) to offer subscriptions across various platforms.
- On YouTube, content creators can offer paid memberships to access exclusive content.
