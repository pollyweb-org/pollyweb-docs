In 2023, Reuters published the following video, titled *"Amazon launches $5 monthly subscription RxPass"*.

- The video reports Amazon's launch of RxPass, a $5 monthly subscription for Prime members. 
- RxPass covers door-step delivery of over 50 generic medications for common conditions like high blood pressure and diabetes. 
- Other Amazon subscriptions include: Prime (2005), Prime Video (2006), AWS (2006), Subscribe & Save (2007), Audible (2008), Kindle Unlimited (2014), and Music Unlimited (2016).
