In 2022, Apple Explained published the following video, titled *"Why Walmart Doesn't Accept Apple Pay"*.

- The video explains why the United States' most popular store, Walmart, does not accept Apple Pay on their stores. 
- The reason is that Walmart has its own payment system, called Walmart Pay, that helps them collect customer data and remove card processing fees.
