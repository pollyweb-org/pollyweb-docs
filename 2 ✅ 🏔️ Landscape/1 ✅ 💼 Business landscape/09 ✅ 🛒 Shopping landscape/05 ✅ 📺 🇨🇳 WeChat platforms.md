In 2016, WeChat released the following video, titled *"WeChat's Four Platforms for Businesses and Developers"*.

- The video talks about WeChat's offerings for businesses, developers, and partners. 
- It mentions WeChat's four platforms: 
    1. Official Accounts Platform to send content and services to followers; 
    1. Open Platform to connect apps or web sites to WeChat's mobile services like authentication and payment;
    1. Hardware Platform to allow users to connect WeChat to their devices like smart watches and smart toys;
    1. Smart Platform for artificial intelligence tools like image, video, and audio recognition.
