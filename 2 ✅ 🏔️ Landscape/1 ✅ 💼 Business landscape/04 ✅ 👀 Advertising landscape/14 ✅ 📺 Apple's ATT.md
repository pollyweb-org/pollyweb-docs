In 2021, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) published the following video, titled *"Apple vs. Facebook: Why iOS 14.5 Started a Big Tech Fight | WSJ"*.

- The video is about the conflict between Apple and Facebook over Apple's iOS 14.5 update, which included a feature called App Tracking Transparency (ATT). 
- ATT requires apps to ask users for permission to track their activity across other apps and websites before they can share that data with other companies. 
- Facebook opposes ATT, arguing that it hurts small businesses and developers.
- Apple defends ATT, saying that it gives users more control over their privacy.
