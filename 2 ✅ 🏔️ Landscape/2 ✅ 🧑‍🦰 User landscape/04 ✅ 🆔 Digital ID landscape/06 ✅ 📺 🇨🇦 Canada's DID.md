In 2023, the Digital ID & Authentication Council of Canada (DIACC) released the following video titled *"Digital ID 101"*.

- The video talks about the benefits of digital identification (ID) in Canada, including saving governments money, helping businesses grow, and giving Canadians more control over their personal information. 
- It also discusses the importance of trust in digital ID and introduces the Pan-Canadian Trust Framework (PCTF).
