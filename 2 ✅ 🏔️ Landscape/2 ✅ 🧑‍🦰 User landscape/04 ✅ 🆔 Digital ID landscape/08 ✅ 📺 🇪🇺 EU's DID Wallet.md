In 2023, the European Commission published the following video, titled *"The European Digital Identity Wallet is coming"*.

- The video introduces the European Union's digital identity system called the EU Digital Identity Wallet.
- As part of the European Digital Identity (EUDI) framework, it aims to provide EU citizens, residents, and businesses with a secure and interoperable digital identity across all member states. 
- Member states are working to implement this system by aligning it with the eIDAS Regulation (electronic Identification, Authentication, and Trust Services).
