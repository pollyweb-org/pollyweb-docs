In 2024, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security published an article with the following video, titled *"U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services Digitizing Immigration Benefits"*.

- The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) plans to offer digital versions of Permanent Resident Cards, complementing physical credentials. 
- This initiative leverages W3C Verifiable Credentials and Decentralized Identifiers standards, enabling individuals to control their data and selectively disclose information.
