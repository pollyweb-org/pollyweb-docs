In 2021, the Government Technology Agency of Singapore publish the following video, titled *"Singpass is even better now"*.

- Singpass is a public digital identity service provided by the government of Singapore which allows residents to access government and business services online. 
- It was launched in 2003, and can now be used to access a variety of services, including: applying for a passport, paying taxes, and booking medical appointments.
