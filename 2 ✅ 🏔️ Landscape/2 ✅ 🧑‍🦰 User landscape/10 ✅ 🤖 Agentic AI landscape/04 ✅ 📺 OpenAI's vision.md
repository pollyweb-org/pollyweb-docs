In 2019, OpenAI published the following video, titled *"Multi-Agent Hide and Seek"*.

- The video discusses how artificial intelligence agents can learn complex behaviors through a simple game of hide-and-seek. 
- The agents, using reinforcement learning, develop strategies like tool use, collaboration, and counter-strategies in response to their opponents' evolving tactics.
