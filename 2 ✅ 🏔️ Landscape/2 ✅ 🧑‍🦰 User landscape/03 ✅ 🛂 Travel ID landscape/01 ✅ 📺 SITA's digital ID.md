In 2023, SITA (a multinational IT provider of 90% of the air transport industry) released the following video, titled *"Travel Digital Identity Solution"*.

- The video highlights the frustrations of traditional air travel, including long queues at check-in and border control. 
- It then introduces a digital travel solution that streamlines the process, allowing passengers to complete tasks (e.g., visa applications and boarding pass retrieval) through their smartphones, resulting in a faster and more enjoyable travel experience.
