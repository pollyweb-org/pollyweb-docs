1. **How does Google Wallet works?**

In the following 2024 video, titled *"Everything you need to know about Google Pay & Wallet"*, Google explains in depth how Google Wallet works.

- The video talks about the Google Wallet API, its new features, and how it can be used to store and manage digital valuables like boarding passes, event tickets, and loyalty cards.
