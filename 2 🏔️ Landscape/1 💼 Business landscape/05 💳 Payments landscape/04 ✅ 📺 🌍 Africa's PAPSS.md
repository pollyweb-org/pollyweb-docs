In 2023, the African Union released the following video entitled *"How the Pan-African Payment and Settlement System #PAPSS works"*. 

- The video introduces the Pan-African Payment and Settlement System (PAPSS), a centralized financial infrastructure designed to enable instant payments across African countries.
- Is also explains PAPSS aims to reduce costs, and accelerate the settlement and payment of trade transactions across African borders, allowing businesses across the continent to saving $5B in transactions costs each year.
