In 2020, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) published the following video, titled *"Why Big Tech Is Getting Into Finance | WSJ"*.

- The video discusses how big tech companies like Apple, Google, and Facebook are entering the financial services industry. 
- These companies are offering services like credit cards, digital wallets, and even their own cryptocurrency. 
- The move is driven by the desire to collect more user data and expand their market dominance, which raises concerns about privacy, competition, and systemic risk.
