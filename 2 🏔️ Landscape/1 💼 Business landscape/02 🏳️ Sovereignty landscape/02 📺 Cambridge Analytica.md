In 2018, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) published the following video, titled *"The Key to Understanding Facebook's Current Crisis"*.

- The video examines the global ramifications of the Cambridge Analytica scandal, particularly its influence on democratic processes in various nations. 
- In 2016, Cambridge Analytica harvested data from millions of Facebook users without consent, impacting political events such as the United States presidential election and the United Kingdom's Brexit referendum. 
- The unauthorized use of personal data for political profiling raised significant concerns about data privacy and the integrity of democratic elections worldwide.
