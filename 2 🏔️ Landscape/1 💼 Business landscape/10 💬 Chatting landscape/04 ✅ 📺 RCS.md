In 2022, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) published the following video, titled *"The Apple, Google and RCS Debate, Explained in Three Minutes"*.

- The video explains the debate between Google and Apple over RCS, a new messaging standard. 
- Back then, Google wanted Apple to adopt RCS to improve texting experiences across different operating systems, eventually benefiting businesses and consumers.
- Apple integrated RCS in iOS 18 in 2024, yet still missing end-to-end encryption for RCS messages exchanged between iPhones and Android devices.
