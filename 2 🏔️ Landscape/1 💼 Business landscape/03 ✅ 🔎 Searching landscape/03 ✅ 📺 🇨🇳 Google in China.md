In 2017, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) published the following video, titled *"Seven Years Without Google in China"*.

- The video is about Google's absence in China, since leaving China in 2010 due to government censorship and cyber attacks. 
- The video talks about Google's attempts to re-enter the Chinese market, such as through its artificial intelligence program AlphaGo, but these efforts have been met with challenges.
