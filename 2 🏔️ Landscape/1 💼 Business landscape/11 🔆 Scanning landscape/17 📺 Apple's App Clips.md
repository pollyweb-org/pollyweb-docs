In 2020, Apple released the following video about App Clips on iOS 14.

- The video introduces App Clips, a new feature in iOS 14 that allows users to quickly access small parts of apps without having to download the full app. 
- It highlights various scenarios where App Clips can be useful, such as paying for parking, ordering coffee, and renting scooters.
