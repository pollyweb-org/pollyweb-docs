In 2023, WhatsApp release the following video, titled *"About accepting payments on the WhatsApp Business app"*.

- In the video, Meta demonstrates how to receive payments in Brazil using WhatsApp for Business.

- The solution relies on Meta Pay (former Facebook Pay), allowing users to send money, shop, and donate across Meta platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. 

- This feature is supported in Brazil and India, working with these countries' central payment systems: PIX in Brazil and UPI in India.

- The platform aims to simplify and secure digital transactions, integrating more seamlessly into Meta's broader ecosystem, including its push toward conversation commerce and the metaverse.
