- Inversion of control in the available payment options;

- Inversion of control in financial fees;
    - i.e., consumers are offered all payment options with different financial fees, and will assume responsibility for the additional fees;

This inversion of control requires the understanding of several market forces, e.g.:

- technology available on smartphones (e.g., contactless);
- existing legal agreements with and between banks and card brands;
- existing central government-own platforms, like in Brazil and India;
- successful adoption of new interfaces by consumers and businesses.
