In 2019, CBS News (a U.S.-based news organization) published the following video, titled *"Google reportedly collecting personal health records in Project Nightingale"*.

- The video discusses Google's "Project Nightingale," a program where Google is reportedly collecting personal health records of millions of Americans from Ascension, a large health system.
- The video explores the privacy concerns surrounding this data collection and the role of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) in protecting patient information.
