2019, The Royal Society (a UK-based the scientific academy) published the following video, titled *"An introduction to neural interfaces | The Royal Society"*.

- The video provides an overview of brain-computer interfaces, which are devices that connect the brain or nervous system to external equipment. 
- It highlights their potential applications, such as assisting individuals with neurological conditions, and discussed both the benefits and ethical considerations associated with these technologies.
