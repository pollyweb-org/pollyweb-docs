In 2023, the Wall Street Journal (a U.S.-based news media) released the following video, titled *"Why Tap-to-Pay Is Safer Than a Credit Card Swipe | WSJ Tech Behind"*.

- The video shows that, from Apple iPhones to New York City subway turnstiles, tap-to-pay use in everyday American life is growing, thanks in part to its security and ease of use. 
- By implementing contactless payments, financial institutions have removed the need for a pin-number on electronic payments with bank cards.
