In 2024, Gartner published the following video, titled *"Digital Twins: Do You Really Know What a Customer Is?"*.

- The video discusses the concept of a digital twin of a customer, which is a virtual representation of a customer based on past interactions. 
- The speakers discuss how this concept can be used to improve customer experience and personalization. 
- They also discuss the challenges of implementing this concept, such as data privacy and security.
