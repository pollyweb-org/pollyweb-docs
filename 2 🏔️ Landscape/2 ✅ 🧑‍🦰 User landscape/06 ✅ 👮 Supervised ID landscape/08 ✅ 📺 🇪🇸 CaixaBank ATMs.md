In 2019, CaixaBank in Spain released the following video, titled "How facial recognition works at CaixaBank ATMs".

- The video shows CaixaBank's facial recognition technology, which allows customers to withdraw cash without inserting a bank card or entering a PIN. 
- To use this service, customers must first register their biometric data at a CaixaBank branch, choosing to authenticate transactions using facial recognition or opt for traditional methods like card and PIN entry.
