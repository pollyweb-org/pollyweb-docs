In 2021, CNET Highlights (a channel dedicated to live tech events and product launches) published the following video, title *"Inside the APPLE HOME! (full WWDC21 HomeKit reveal)"*.

- The video discusses Apple's new HomeKit features, as announced at WWDC 2021 - Apple's most important event of the year.
- The features include: Home Keys for keyless entry, Shared Play for group TV watching, and expanded Siri support on third-party devices. 
- It also highlights improvements to the Home app, such as intercom and enhanced camera controls.
