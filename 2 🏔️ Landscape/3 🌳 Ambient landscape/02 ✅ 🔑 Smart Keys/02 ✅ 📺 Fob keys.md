In 2020, BioConnect (a security technology company) published the following video, titled *"Can a FOB be duplicated?"*.

- The video demonstrates how easily a key fob can be duplicated. The narrator purchases a duplicate fob from a local shop for a small fee and successfully uses it to access a building. 
- This highlights the vulnerability of traditional key fob systems and the need for additional security measures, such as mobile authenticators.
