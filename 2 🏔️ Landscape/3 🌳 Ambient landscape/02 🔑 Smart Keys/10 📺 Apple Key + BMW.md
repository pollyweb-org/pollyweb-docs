In 2020, CNET Highlights (a channel dedicated to live tech events and product launches) published the following video from Apple, titled *"Car Key! Unlocks BMW 5 Series with an iPhone (Full Car Play reveal)"*.

- This video is about the new Car Key feature in iOS 14. 
- It allows users to unlock and start their car using an iPhone. 
- Users can also share their car keys with others using iMessage. 
- iPhone's Power Reserve mode allows users to open doors up to 5 hours after they ran out of battery on their phones.
