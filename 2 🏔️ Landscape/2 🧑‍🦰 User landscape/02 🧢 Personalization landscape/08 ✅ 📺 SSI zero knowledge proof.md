In 2021, Animo (a U.S.-based YouTube channel that creates educational videos about technology) published the following video, titled *"Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) Explained*".

- The video explains Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI), a system that gives individuals control over their personal data. 
- SSI uses a digital wallet to store encrypted credentials, allowing users to selectively share information with companies. 
- This increases privacy, efficiency, and security for both individuals and businesses.
