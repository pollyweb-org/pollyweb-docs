ğŸ’¬ NLWeb sees delegation with the following combination:
- user assistants, responsible for:
    - **user interaction** - speaks with the user in natural language;
    - **user privacy** - serves as a trusted broker to the user's data;
- service providers, responsible for:
    - **implementing services** - keeps online APIs to receive orders/requests;
    - **publishing offers** - publishes the catalog of services provided;
- orchestrators (e.g., Task Rabbit), responsible for:
    - **outcome reasoning** - defines the steps to achieve the final outcome;
    - **business discovery** - searches for potential offers from service providers;
    - **orchestration** - manages the conversation and time line with all parties;
    - **business interaction** - remotely executes the targeted business APIs;
    - **human in the loop** - bridging the gaps where AI is unable to execute;
    - **responsible AI** - establishing legal, social, and moral guardrails.
