In 2023, the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (RGIA) of Hyderabad, India, released the following video titled *"A smoother journey with DigiYatra at #HYDAirport"*.

- The video is about the DigiYatra, an industry-led digital way  to travel paperless and contactless, coordinated by the Ministry of Civil Aviation in India.
- It shows how to use the app to register, check in, and go through security quickly and easily.
