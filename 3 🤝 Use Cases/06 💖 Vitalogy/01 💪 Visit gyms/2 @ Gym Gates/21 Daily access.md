How to go through the gym's gates?
--

> Users can go through gym gates in multiple ways - e.g.:

* by [tapping 🔆](<../../../../4 ⚙️ Solution/25 🔆 Locators/Locators 🔆/🔆 Locator.md>) with their phones
* by tapping their [userables 💍](<../../../../4 ⚙️ Solution/25 🔆 Locators/Userables 💍/💍💠 Userable thing.md>) 
* by scanning their [palms 🖐️](<../../../../4 ⚙️ Solution/60 🧰 Edge/63 🖐️ Palmists/01 🖐️🔌 Palmist device.md>)

> Whatever the method, the gym always sends feedback to the [Wallet 🧑‍🦰](<../../../../4 ⚙️ Solution/20 🧑‍🦰 UI/Wallets 🧑‍🦰/🧑‍🦰 Wallet app/🧑‍🦰 Wallet 🛠️ app.md>).
