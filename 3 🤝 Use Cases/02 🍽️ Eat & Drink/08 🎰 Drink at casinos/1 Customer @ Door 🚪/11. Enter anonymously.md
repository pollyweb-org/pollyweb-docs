Enter anonymously in a casino
---

Users can ask their identity Vault for an age-related token (e.g., over 16 years old). 

* At the casino, if users only have the token printed QR or in an NFC card, users tap/scan the gate - the casino then takes a picture of the user with a fixed camera, and ask the token's identity Vault to match the picture. 

* If users have their wallet device, the casino then opens a chat on the user's wallet, asks to share the token, then takes a picture to send to the token's identity Vault to matching.
